My name's Anupam, and I've been making games for almost 4 years. I made my first game with Construct 2, and I regret using Construct 2 to this day. It was a completely horrible experience. I tried Phaser, and it was pretty good. Gamemaker came next, was a fun engine to use but its language GML isn't object oriented. I found out about haxeflixel and fell in love with it. It's a great framework, and I love it. I'm currently using Unity, it's awesome, but it's UI sucks. I had to spend 15 minutes looking for the Asset Store.
